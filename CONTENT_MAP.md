## **Content Principles (Site-Wide)**

### **Tone & Voice**
- **Professional but approachable**: We're solving a serious problem, but we're not stuffy
- **Community-focused**: Always frame benefits in terms of "you" and "your community"
- **Transparent**: Honest about challenges, targets, and limitations
- **Action-oriented**: Clear CTAs, direct language, benefit-driven messaging

### **Messaging Guidelines**
1. **Lead with benefits** before technical features
2. **Use "you/your"** instead of "we/our" when describing impact
3. **Be specific**: Concrete numbers, real examples, authentic stories
4. **Avoid superlatives**: "Revolutionary" → "Effective"; "Amazing" → "Proven"
5. **Scannable**: Short paragraphs, bullet points, clear headings

### **SEO Considerations**
- **Primary Keywords**: sargassum solutions, coastal waste energy, biochar, carbon-negative agriculture, renewable energy Caribbean
- **Meta Descriptions**: Each page should have unique, benefit-focused meta descriptions
- **Alt Text**: All images must have descriptive, keyword-relevant alt text
